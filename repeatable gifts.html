Repeatable Gifts

These gifts may be purchased any number of times (as opposed to all other gifts, which may only be purchased once each). These gifts also have no requisite skill needed in order to purchase. Due to the high cost of these gifts, players are strongly recommended to wait before purchasing.

Specialization - (Passive)

To Purchase: [10 Gift Points]

Character gains a +5 bonus to one Lore art of choice. This gift costs 2 less gift points if the Lore art they choose has a higher total than any of their other Lore arts.

Fitness - (Passive)

To Purchase: [10 Gift Points]

Character gains +25 to Stamina and +10 to Death Health.

Mettle - (Passive)

To Purchase: [10 Gift Points]

Character gains +5 to Chest, +4 to Leg, +2 to Arm, and +1 to Head Health. This Health added does not affect the characterâ€™s evasion (these Health bonuses increases the density of the character, not their size).

Paradigm Shift- (Passive)

To Purchase: [15 Gift Points]

Character gains +2 to Sanity Regen per day. The character may purchase this for free instead if they choose to take a Coping Mechanism. A Character cannot have more than 2 Coping Mechanisms total. Note: Coping Mechanisms may only be removed by a significant Plot Points purchase.) Some Coping Mechanisms may be purchased twice. Coping Mechanisms are as follows:

Substance Addiction

Character must consume at least two doses of whatever substance they are addicted to daily or lose 2d6 SN (if they consume their substance only once, then this is reduced to 1d6).

Obsession

Character chooses a thing that they are obsessed with (person, item, place, etc). The character must remain within sight, touch, or hearing (this depends on the type of obsession) of their object at all times. For any day where the object was not within sight, touch, or hearing at any point, the character loses 1d6 SN and cannot regenerate SN that day.

Flightiness

Character gains a permanent -20 to Courage. Any failed Courage checks cost the player 2SN.

Impulsiveness

Character gains a permanent -20 to Willpower. Any failed Willpower checks cost the player 2SN.

Passivity

Character gains a permanent -20 to Authority. Any time the character uses all of their PT in a round or has the highest INI in a battle, they lose 2SN.

Emotionally Distant

Character gains a permanent -20 to Charisma. Any time another heals the character, they lose 1SN.

Delusional

Character must choose one lie that they believe as a truth. If the character fails any social maneuver at all they lose 1SN. If the character fails a social maneuver related to the lie they believe, they lose 5SN.

Rest Avoidance

Character gains a permanent -25 to Meditation/Rest.
